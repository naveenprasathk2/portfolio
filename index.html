Approach / 02

Three principles
I design by.

Enterprise software is where most people spend eight hours of their day. These are the non-negotiables that shape every decision I make.

— i.

Clarity over cleverness.

The interface should disappear into the task. If a user notices the design, I've usually failed. Every pixel earns its place, or it goes.

— ii.

Research before pixels.

Opinions are cheap. I anchor every design decision in interviews, usability tests, heuristic reviews, and real operator feedback — not instinct alone.

— iii.

Systems, not screens.

One-off screens are a tax on engineering. I design reusable tokens, components, and patterns so every future feature gets cheaper, not harder.
